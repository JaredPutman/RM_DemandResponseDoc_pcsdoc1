Information to Provide

Please have the following information prepared when contacting Customer Support:

  • Product version number (Help/About menu selection from the application).
  • Know the exact workflow you used that generated the error.
  • Note the exact wording of any error messages you see.
  • Explain how you have tried to solve the problem.

Follow-up Procedures

Once Customer Support is contacted:

  • Your issue is assigned a unique identification number that you can use to track its resolution.
  • A Customer Support team member will be assigned primary ownership.
  • An email acknowledging our receipt of the issue is returned to you within four (4) business hours.
  • Depending on the severity of your issue, a resolution plan will be issued to you within three (3) business days.
  • After your issue is resolved, an email detailing the resolution will be sent to you.
