How to Cancel or Delete a Trip

  1. Navigate to the correct customer, select a date on the calendar (must be in the future), and locate the trip you wish to delete.
  1. Click the trash can icon on the tool bar and select an option from the drop-down window:
  • Cancel trip for – [mm/dd/yyyy only]: Cancel the trip for the selected date only.
  • Delete trip for – [mm/dd/yyyy only]: Delete the trip for the selected date only.
  • Delete trip for this day forward - [All applicable days]: Use this option to delete a standing order.
  1. A warning now displays to confirm your decision to cancel or delete the trip. Click Yes to proceed.
  1. If the trip is chained to other trips, a pop-up will display. Select the trips in this pop-up you would like to cancel along with the original trip by checking the boxes in the Select column. Click Save and Return to compete the cancelation/deletion.
