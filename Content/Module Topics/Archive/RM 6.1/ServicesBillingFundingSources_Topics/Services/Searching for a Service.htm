Searching for a Service

If you need to locate a service, you can browse the list on the left or perform a search by completing the steps below.

  1. Locate the search bar at the top of the screen.
  1. Enter the name of your desired service.
  2. Click the Search button, or tap Enter on your keyboard.
  3. The system then searches through the services list and displays any matches on the screen. You can continue clicking the search button to scroll through all of your results.
