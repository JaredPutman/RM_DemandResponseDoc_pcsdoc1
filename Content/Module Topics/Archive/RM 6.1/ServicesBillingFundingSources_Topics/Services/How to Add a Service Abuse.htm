How to Add a Service Abuse

  1. Locate your desired service and open the Service Abuses tab.

  1. Click the New Item button on the tab’s toolbar. This will add a new row to the screen.
  1. In the new row, select an abuse from the drop-down list. When you select an abuse, the default amount of abuse points and the default grace period will display in the grid.
  1. To assign a different number of points or a different grace period to the service abuse, enter the revised amounts in the Abuse Points (Service) and Grace Period (Service) fields. These fields override the amounts entered in the Default fields and do not affect the other services in your system.
  1. Repeat these steps to add all the necessary service abuses.
  1. Click Save and Return on the tab’s toolbar.

 

If an abuse has a checked box in the Deleted column, this indicates that the abuse was deleted in the Settings module.
