Service Statistics

A service’s statistics display the number of trips scheduled and unscheduled to it for a specific date, as well as the number of vehicles in use. This provides a quick way for your agency to gather this information, which may be beneficial if a service can only have a certain number of trips per day.

  1. Select your desired service.
  1. In the General tab, select a date from the drop-down calendar in the Statistics field.
  1. The field to the right will now populate with this service’s statistics.
