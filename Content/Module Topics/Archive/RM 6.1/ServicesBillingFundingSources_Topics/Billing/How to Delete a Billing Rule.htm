How to Delete a Billing Rule

If you need to delete a billing rule, complete the following steps.

  1. From the Billing module, select a billing rule to delete.
  2. Select a date from the drop-down calendar on the toolbar. If you change the date to a day in the future, the billing rule is not removed until that day is reached.
  3. Click the Delete button on the toolbar.
  4. On the screen that displays, click the Yes button. This removes the billing rule from your system.Chapter 7: Funding Sources

The Funding Sources module is used to create, edit, and delete funding sources for your agency. A funding source and its associated billing rule(s) are used when calculating trip cost for a customer. Various funding sources may be entered in this window, which may include insurance companies, health care institutions, or other funding types. At least one billing rule (usually more) must be created for each funding source.
