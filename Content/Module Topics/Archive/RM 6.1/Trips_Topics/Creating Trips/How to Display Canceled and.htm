How to Display Canceled and Deleted Trips

When you delete or cancel a trip, it no longer appears on the screen. However, if you need to refer to a trip's record after it has been removed, you can do so by completing the following steps:

  1. Navigate to the correct customer and select the date for which you would like to view canceled/deleted trips.
  1. Click the Include Deleted/Canceled Trips button on the right side of the tool bar.
  1. Canceled and deleted trips now appear on the screen. A trip with a red X has been canceled and a trip with a trash can symbol has been deleted.
