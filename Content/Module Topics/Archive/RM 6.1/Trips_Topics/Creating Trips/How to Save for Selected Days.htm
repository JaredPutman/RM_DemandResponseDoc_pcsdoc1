How to Save for Selected Days Forward

If you select the Save trip for - [mm/dd/yyyy only] and selected days forward option when saving, you must also assign the appropriate dates to the trip.

  1. When you select this option, the Save Trip To Multiple Dates screen appears.

  1. On this screen, click the trip's applicable dates on the calendar to add it to the Selected Dates column (the limit is thirteen). If you make a mistake, it is possible to remove a single selected trip, or all of them at once with the corresponding buttons.
  1. When you have all of the correct dates selected, click Save.
