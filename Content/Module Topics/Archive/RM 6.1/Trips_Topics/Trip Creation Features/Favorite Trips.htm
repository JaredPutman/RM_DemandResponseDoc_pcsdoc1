Favorite Trips

This feature allows you to use a customer's previously-configured favorite trip as the basis for a new trip. Favorite trips are typically those that a customer takes often, so this feature can save you the redundancy of creating the same trip from scratch every time the customer needs to take it. A customer's favorite trips are assigned in the Customers module; please refer to the Customer's guide for more information.

  1. Navigate to the correct customer and click New.
  1. Click on the Favorite Trips… button located next to the Previous Trips button. The Favorite Trips screen then appears.

  1. Select one of the customer’s favorite trips.
  1. Click Select to populate the Trips screen with the favorite trip's information. You can now proceed with the trip creation process.
