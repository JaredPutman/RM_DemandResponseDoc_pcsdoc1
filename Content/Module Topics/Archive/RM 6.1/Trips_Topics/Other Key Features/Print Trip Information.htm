Print Trip Information

  1. Navigate to the correct customer, select a date on the calendar, and find the trip you wish to print.
  1. Click the arrow next to the Print button on the tool bar.
  1. From the drop-down list, choose to print out either the trip audit log or the trip’s information.
  1. A screen displays which is populated with the information you requested. From here, you can print out a copy by clicking on the button.
