Show Recommendations

Show Recommendations allows you to run RSE on a trip and view suggestions for where in the schedule to insert the trip. Complete the following steps to run the Show Recommendations tool:

  1. Select a trip.
  1. Click the Optimize button on the toolbar and select the Show Recommendations option from the drop-down menu.
  1. On the screen that loads, click the Show Recommendations button on the toolbar. A list of the vehicles, runs, and times available for inserting the trip appears at the bottom of the screen. These vehicles are sorted based on the impact the trip's insertion will have on the current schedule.

 

Vehicles Sorted by Impact means that the most suitable vehicle for this trip is listed at the top of the list. The vehicle impact factor is displayed by color bullets: blue, green, yellow, and red in that order with blue representing the best option and red representing the least desirable option.

  1. If needed, click the Show Messages button at the top of the screen to view any errors or messages pertaining to the trip.
  1. Select a vehicle from the list.
  1. Click the Schedule Trip button and select a scheduling option.
