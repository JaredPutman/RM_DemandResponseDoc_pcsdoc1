Optimizing Schedules with Runs

Runs can also be optimized with RSE. You can optimize a single run or several runs at the same time. RSE also allows you add trips to your exiting run or add trips around your runs.

  1. To optimize the trips on a run, select the run for optimization during the vehicle selection process.
  1. Select the Advanced optimization method and then select Schedule Trips within runs.

  1. Click the Optimize button.
  1. Notice the schedule before (Current Schedule) and the schedule after (Proposed Schedule) in the diagram below.

You can also add trips to an existing run by selecting the Advanced optimization method and selecting: Keep scheduled trips on same vehicles, Keep scheduled trips at same time, Attempt to insert unscheduled trips, and Schedule trips within runs.

 

The trips added to the run during the optimization. None of the existing trips was changed. If you do not select Keep scheduled trips at the same time this may allow additional trips to be added since the original trips times may be altered but the trips will not be removed from the run.
