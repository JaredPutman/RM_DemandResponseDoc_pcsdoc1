Commit the Schedule

After you have created and adjusted your schedule, you can commit the schedule for a specific vehicle. Committing the schedule copies the schedule to the future dates selected. If there are items already on future schedules, RouteMatch attempts to reconcile the two schedules. If there is a conflict, the existing trips are left as-is and the new scheduled trips are not added. Exceptions are not copied to future dates.

  1. In the Scheduled Trips area, select the vehicle(s) that you want to commit to the schedule. When viewed using Enhanced Manual Scheduling, click a vehicle in the Vehicle List area.
  1. Click the Commit Schedule button on the toolbar and select an option from the drop down list.

  1. Choose an option from the list:
  • Commit Schedule for this day forward [day only] - The day is the day of the week for the currently selected date. So, for example, if the day was Saturday, the schedule would be set for every Saturday from this day forward.
  • Commit Schedule for this day forward [all applicable days] - Applies the schedule for this day to all future days that this trip occurs.
