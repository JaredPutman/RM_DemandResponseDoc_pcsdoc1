Delete a Break

You can delete a break time by completing the following steps.

  1. In the Scheduled Trips area, click (plus sign) beside the vehicle to display the vehicle breaks. When viewed using Enhanced Manual Scheduling, click a vehicle in the Vehicle List area.
  1. Click on the desired break.
  1. Click (Delete/Cancel button) on the toolbar. Select a delete option:
  • Delete break for
  • Delete break for this day forward – [Requested weekdays only]
  1. Select OK.
