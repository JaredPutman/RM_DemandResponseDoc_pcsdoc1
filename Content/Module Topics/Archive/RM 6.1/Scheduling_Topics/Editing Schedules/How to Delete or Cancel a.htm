How to Delete or Cancel a Trip or Run

  1. Expand the appropriate lists and select the trip or run you wish to remove.
  1. Click (Delete/Cancel button) and select how you wish to cancel or delete this trip. There are three options:
  • Cancel trip for – [mm/dd/yyyy only] – Cancel the trip for the selected date only.
  • Delete trip for – [mm/dd/yyyy only] – Delete the trip for the selected date only.
  • Delete trip to vehicle for this day forward – [All applicable days] – Delete the trip for the selected date and for all applicable days forward.
  1. Click Yes in the warning message that displays to cancel or delete the trip.
