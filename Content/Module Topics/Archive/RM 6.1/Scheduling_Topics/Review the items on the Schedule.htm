1. Review the items on the Schedule Validation screen.
  1. If there are more than 25 items total, paging controls appear at the bottom of the screen. The single right-facing arrow pages to the next 25 items. The double right-facing arrows show the last 25 items. The single left-facing arrow pages to the previous 25 items. The double left-facing arrow pages to the first 25 items.

  1. You can filter the list of items to see only the category that you are interested in. You can only filter a list that has more than one category represented. To filter, click the down arrow on the filtering menu in the bottom left corner of the screen and select a category. Select All to view items in all categories.
  1. If necessary, manually solve items. You can perform one of the suggestions listed under Here is a way to solve this on your own on the Schedule Validation window or another solution of your choice.
  1. To have RouteMatch take you to the item, right-click the item on the Schedule Validation window and select Take me to this trip in Scheduling from the pop-up menu. It is easier to see the trip if you do not have the Enhanced Manual Scheduling view enabled.

  1. To return to the Schedule Validation window, click the Validate Schedule button again.
  1. If necessary, have the system automatically solve items. Click the appropriate check box under Here is a way we can automatically solve this for you on the Schedule Validation window.

Notes

You can select the automatic solution checkbox for multiple items before moving on to the next step, which is applying these solutions. If you want to use the paging controls to select checkboxes for more items, you will have to complete the next step before you can page.

Some items will include the automatic solution: Consider this item valid. You can mark as valid the items that you have decided not to solve. However, selecting this checkbox and applying this solution does not change anything about the item on the schedule. It is simply a way for you to keep track of which of these items you have decided not to solve.

  1. Click Apply. The word "Resolved" appears if the items were solved, either manually or automatically. If the item was simply marked as valid but not solved, the word "Acknowledged" appears under the icon. You can unacknowledge an item by clicking the Consider this item valid and clicking Apply again. Clicking Apply does not renumber items.

  1. To unclutter the screen, you can hide the items that you have acknowledged as valid by clicking the Hide Items Marked Valid checkbox. Click the checkbox again to show these items.
  1. Click Refresh to reload the screen. The items are renumbered. Any items that were resolved no longer appear on the screen. If you did not resolve an item, it still appears on the screen, whether or not you acknowledged it as valid. This is because the system is letting you know that it is still finding the item.
  1. Close the Schedule Validation window when you are finished.
