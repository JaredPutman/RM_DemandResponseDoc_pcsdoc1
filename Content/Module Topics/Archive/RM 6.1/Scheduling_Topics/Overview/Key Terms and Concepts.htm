Key Terms and Concepts

Term or Concept Definition

Constraints

Factors and settings that limit which trips can be scheduled on an available vehicle.

Optimization

Using RSE to schedule multiple trips onto available vehicles, taking into account costs and constraints.

Runs

Blocks of time on a vehicle schedule for a specific purpose.

Scheduling

Adding trips to available vehicles to create an efficient list of passenger pick-ups and drop-offs.

Show Recommendations

Schedule a single trip as it would fit onto available vehicles that a scheduler can choose from to add the trip to.
