You can also add trips around an existing run by selecting the Advanced optimization method and selecting: Keep scheduled trips on same vehicles, Keep scheduled trips at same time, Attempt to insert unscheduled trips, and Schedule trips around runs.

Note

If you do not want your Garage Pull-in and Pull-out Times to be adjusted during optimization, set the Miscellaneous Scheduling Options setting in the Settings Module > Scheduling Options to "0"

Note

The trips added around the run during the optimization. None of the existing trips were changed.
