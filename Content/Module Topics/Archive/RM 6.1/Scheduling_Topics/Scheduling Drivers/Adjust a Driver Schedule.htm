Adjust a Driver Schedule

After scheduling a driver, you can adjust the driver’s schedule.

  1. In the Scheduled Trips areas, select a Vehicle.
  1. Click Schedule driver(s) for the selected vehicle button) on the Scheduling window toolbar. The Driver Scheduling window appears.
  1. Select the scheduled driver in the Driver Scheduling area.
  1. Click and the Change Time screen appears.

  1. Adjust the Start Time, if applicable.
  1. Adjust the End Time, if applicable.
  1. Click Ok.
  1. Click and select a save option:
  • Use this schedule for today [mm/dd/yyyy] only
  • Use this schedule for today and all [day of week] in the future
