Scheduling

In the Scheduling module, vehicle modes are easily recognizable by a new set of icons. You can also see fixed-route stops on your "mixed mode" vehicles, making it easy to manually assign paratransit runs and trips where they fit in your fixed-routes.

This icon indicates that the vehicle is a mixed mode vehicle. Fixed-route runs and paratransit runs and trips can be added to this type of vehicle.

This icon indicates that the run is a fixed-route run.

This icon marks the trip as a fixed-route trip.

This icon shows that the stop is a fixed-route stop.

 

Vehicles marked as fixed-route do not show up in scheduling. Only paratransit and "mixed mode" vehicles can be seen and scheduled to. To configure fixed-routes, you must use RouteMatch Fixed Route modules.

You can also use RSE to place schedule the trips to your mixed mode vehicles as if the fixed-route stops were paratransit standing orders. The fixed-route stops stay in place and the paratransit trips are placed where they fit.

 

To schedule paratransit and fixed-route trips to the same vehicle, the Flex Route Operation option in the Scheduling Options of the Settings module must be set to True. This has to be done by a system administrator directly in the database. The setting found in RouteMatch is read-only.
