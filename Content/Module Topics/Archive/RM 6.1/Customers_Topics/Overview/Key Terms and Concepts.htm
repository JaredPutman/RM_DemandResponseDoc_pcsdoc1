Key Terms and Concepts

Term or Concept Definition

 Attendant

A person who has been certified to accompany a customer

 Mobility Type

A description of how the customer moves around.

 Ambulatory

A mobility type in which the customer can get around without any aids

 Official Name

The actual name of the customer if a nickname is used in the Name fields

 Customer

Someone who uses your service for going from one destination to another.
