How to perform a Quick Lookup

  1. On the Quick Lookup screen, enter your search criteria in the Reservation/Customer box.
  2. Select the type of search you want to make.
  3. Click Lookup. If you are searching on the Last Name and multiple customers match that name or partial name, the Customer Lookup dialog appears, allowing you to select the customer.

  1. Review the returned trips in your Trip Records list. Select the trip you want to view or edit.
