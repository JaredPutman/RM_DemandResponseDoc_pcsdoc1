AVL Display & Map Options

The Automatic Vehicle Locator (AVL) Display tab allows a dispatcher to locate their agency's vehicles on the map in real-time.  The AVL map displays the vehicle's current location based on the GPS information reported by the agency's wireless devices.  Vehicle icons appear on the map to indicate where the driver is on their route.  These icons move on the map as the vehicles progress through the stops.  This way a dispatcher can know where in their service area any vehicle is currently located.
