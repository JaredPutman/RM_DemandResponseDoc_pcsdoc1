You can show or hide the color coding legend by clicking on the ETA Legend button on the toolbar.

Legend is not visible. Click to show.

Legend is visible. Click to hide.

 

When ETA (early/late) shows a green or yellow box, the number in the column is always 0 for the minutes the vehicle is early or late. Those colors indicate that the vehicle is on-time, but running ahead or behind.
