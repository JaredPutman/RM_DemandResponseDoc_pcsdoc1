Conflicting Data Views

Since the Stop/Trip mode option is permanent, three issues can arise as you use the Dispatching module:

  1. If you choose to combine date views (by holding the control button on the keyboard) with different Stop/Trip Modes, a warning will display to stop you from doing so.

  1. If you switch to a data view that has a different Stop/Trip mode, you will need to save your progress on the original data view before moving on to the new one.

  1. If you close a data view with a different Stop/Trip Mode than the Whiteboard screen, then the Whiteboard screen will display automatically. You will also be prompted to save your work.
