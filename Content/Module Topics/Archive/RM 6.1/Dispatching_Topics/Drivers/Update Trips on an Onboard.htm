Update Trips on an Onboard Device

A dispatcher may need to update the trips displayed on the manifest of an onboard device. You can update trips using the resending/synchronized/initialized feature. This feature is available if you are licensed for wireless.

 

 How to resend trips to Onboard Device

If a trip does not appear on the manifest of an onboard device, a dispatcher can resend the trip to the device.

  1. Select a trip or multiple trips from the trip list in the Dispatching window.
  2. Click (MDC Option-Resend Trips button) on the toolbar.
  3. Select Resend from the drop-down list.
  4. Close the success message that appears.

  1. Click the Refresh button on the toolbar if the Dispatching window does not automatically refresh.
  2. The driver will receive a Manifest Update message with the trip confirmation number and what was changed. The driver will need to tap OK to clear the screen.

 

 How to synchronize the Dispatching screen and onboard device

The manifest of an onboard device may be out of synchronization with, or display something different from, the Dispatching screen. A dispatcher can synchronize the device with the Dispatching screen.

  1. Select a trip or multiple trips from the trip list in the Dispatching window.
  2. Click (MDC Option-Resend Trips button) on the toolbar.
  3. Select Synchronize from the drop-down list.
