Verification Fields

In this appendix, you will find brief definitions of each of the Verification fields, instructions on how to change or add information to them, and where in RouteMatch the information for that field was originally entered. If you are using Trip Mode, several of these fields will be split into separate pickup (PU) and drop-off (DO) fields.

Name of Field

Definition of Field

How to Edit the Field’s Information

Location of Original Information

 ADA ID

The customer’s ADA (American’s with Disability Act) ID number

Click on this field and enter the ID number. The field is located on the Custom Fields tab.

Customers Module

 Additional Charge

Anything the customer paid in addition to the normal fare. 

Click on this field and enter an amount of money.

Dispatching Module

 Amount Reimbursed

Amount of money refunded to the customer.

Click on this field and enter an amount of money.

Dispatching Module

 Analysis Note

Shows odometer and trip time discrepancies within the verification grid in red text.

This field cannot be edited.

Verification Module

 APC Passengers Off

The number of passengers who exited the vehicle as recorded by the Automatic Passenger Counter (APC).

This field cannot be edited.

APC Device

 APC Passengers On

The number of passengers who boarded the vehicle as recorded by the Automatic Passenger Counter (APC).

This field cannot be edited.

APC Device

Any special assistance your customer needed for the trip. 

Click on the ellipsis (…) in this field to bring up the Edit Trip Assistance Needs window and add any missing needs.

Customers Module

 Attendant Mobility

The mobility type of the attendant(s) accompanying your customer.

Customers Module

 Attendants

The number of attendants that accompanied the customer on the trip.

Click on this field and enter a number.

Customers module

 Authorization

The billing authorization for the trip.

Click on the ellipsis (…) in this field to display the Authorization Lookup screen and make a selection.

Billing Module

 Billing Category

The category of billing rules applied to this trip.

Click on the field and then the down arrow to open the drop-down list and make a selection. These are defined in the settings under Trip Options.

Trips Module

 Billing data in dispute

Indicates that the customer disputes the amount they were billed.

Click the checkbox if this occurred.

Dispatching Module

 Cancellation

Indicates that the trip was cancelled by the customer.

Click the checkbox if this occurred.

Dispatching Module

 Cancellation Type

Indicates the type of cancellation made by the customer.

Can be Regular Cancel, Late Cancel, or Late Cancel – Pay. The last type is for when an agency requires payment for late cancellations.

Use the drop-down list to select a type.

Verification Module

The number of car seats entered for the trip.

Click on this field and enter a number.

Trips Module

 Completed

Indicates that verification has been completed for the current leg of the trip. Use this field to keep track of your progress.

Be aware that you must also complete the opposite leg of the trip. It is not completed automatically.

Click the checkbox if this has occurred.

Dispatching Module

 Confirmation#

The number automatically assigned to a trip by the software when it is created.

This field cannot be edited.

Trips Module

The group that was used to coordinate the trip.
