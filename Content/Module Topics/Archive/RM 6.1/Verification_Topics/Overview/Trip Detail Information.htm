Trip Detail Information Bar

Many of the tools associated with the Verification module allow you to add and change various pieces of information to insure that your reports are as accurate as possible.

The information on the trip detail bar is automatically updated every time a save with / without verification occurs. The software takes into account the settings you have established under the verification options and the information entered in the trip details each time the bar updates. Double check this information before saving with validation to make sure there are not any obvious mistakes. The fields of the information bar are defined as follows:

 

  • Customer Pay Collected – The cash and pass values physically collected by the driver for each trip
  • Customer Pay Expected – The customer pay value that was printed on the driver manifest for that trip.
  • Customer Pay Adjusted – The customer pay value after all trip details have been corrected. Cancellations, no shows, guests/attendants, changes to funding source, etc. all impact this value as these can be day of trip changes.
  • Cost to Funding Source – The funding source cost value.
  • Cost of Trip – The customer cost plus the funding source cost.
  • Revenue Mileage – The miles traveled during the run that translate into revenue for your agency.
  • Service Mileage – The total miles traveled during the run.
  • Revenue Time – The amount of time (in hours) taken to carry out the run that translates into revenue for your agency.

  • Service Time – The total amount of time (in hours) taken to carry out the run.
