How to Assign Drivers to Runs and Vehicles

  1. Select the Run Management button from the Verification Module tool bar.
  2. Click the Assign Run/Driver tab.
  1. Use the View button to display the Drivers Pane and be sure to refresh the window to include the most up-to-date information.


  1. Find the driver you wish to assign by browsing through the Drivers pane or search for the driver’s name.
  1. Select the driver.
     
  1. Find and select either a vehicle or run from the Vehicle And Assigned Runs pane.
  1. Click the Assign button and choose to either assign the driver to all of the trips associated with the run/vehicle or a portion of the runs. Once you make the assignment, the driver’s name will appear in parenthesis next to the run/vehicle. It is possible to assign multiple drivers to each run and vehicle.

From this screen, you can also unassign runs and drivers from vehicles:

  1. Select a vehicle.
     
  1. Click on the Unassign button and choose to Unassign Run, Unassign Driver All (of the runs), or Unassign Driver (from a portion of the runs).

When you are done managing the runs and drivers, close the window to return to Verification.
