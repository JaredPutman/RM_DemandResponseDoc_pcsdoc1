Customer and Trip Details

Complete the following steps to view the full details of a trip.

  1. Right-click on a trip.
  2. On the menu that appears, select the Detail option. A small window will appear containing customer and trip details.
  3. Be sure to leave the mouse stationary while the window is displayed. Moving the mouse will remove the window.

If you are looking for a piece of information that is not contained in this window, right-click the trip and select the Properties option. This will load the trip in the Trips module.
