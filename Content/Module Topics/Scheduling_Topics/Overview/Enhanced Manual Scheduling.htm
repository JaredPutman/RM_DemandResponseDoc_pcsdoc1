Enhanced Manual Scheduling

The Enhanced Manual Scheduling sub-window is an alternative view of the Scheduled Trips sub-window. It displays all scheduled activity for the vehicles selected from the Vehicle List in a calendar format. You can display up to 8 vehicles at a time. Enhanced Manual Scheduling can help you visualize the schedule with the following display features:

  • Calendar view breaks down the vehicle manifests into 15-minute intervals.
  • Runs are displayed as a strip along the left side of the calendar for each vehicle.
  • Trips and breaks are displayed at the times they occur on the calendar.
  • Times before garage pull-out and after garage pull-in are shaded in blue.
