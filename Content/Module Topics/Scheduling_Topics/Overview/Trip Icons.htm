Trip Icons

The Scheduling module contains a set of icons to indicate the various types of items found in the window. The icons you see depend on your system configuration. For example, if your agency has fixed route and demand response services, you may see fixed route trip icons in this window. The icons identify their associated item and include various elements to provide additional information.

Additionally, the font and color of the label text provide information about the vehicle, trip, or run. Click the Hide or Show The Legend button to view the full legend of icons.
