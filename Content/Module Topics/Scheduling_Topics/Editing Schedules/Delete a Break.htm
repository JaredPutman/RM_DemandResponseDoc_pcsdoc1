Delete a Break

You can delete a break time by completing the following steps.

  1. In the Scheduled Trips area, click the plus sign beside the vehicle to display the vehicle breaks. When viewed using Enhanced Manual Scheduling, click a vehicle in the Vehicle List area.
  2. Click on the desired break.
  3. Click the Delete/Cancel button on the toolbar and select a delete option:
  • Delete break for
  • Delete break for this day forward – [Requested weekdays only]
  1. Select OK.
