Change Vehicle Garage Stop Properties

You can change the vehicle garage stop properties by completing the following steps:

  1. In the Scheduled Trips area, click the plus sign next to the vehicle to display the vehicle garage stops. A garage stop is indicated by a key icon and includes the time.
  2. Right-click on the garage stop that you want to edit.

  1. Select Properties from the menu that appears. The Garage Stop Properties screen displays.

  1. Make changes to the garage stop.
  2. Click Save to save the garage stop properties for the vehicle.
