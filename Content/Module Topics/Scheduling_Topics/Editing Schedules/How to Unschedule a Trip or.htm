How to Unschedule a Trip or Run

Complete the following steps to unschedule a trip or a run. When you unschedule a run, all of its associated trips are also unscheduled.

  1. Locate and select the item you want to unschedule.
  2. You have two options to remove the item from the schedule:
  • Click the Unschedule button on the toolbar.
  • Drag the trip or run off of the vehicle and drop it on one of the unscheduled sub-windows.
  1. Select an unscheduling option.
  2. The trip or run is no longer scheduled to the vehicle.
