Select Trip or Stop

When creating schedules, you are able to toggle between selecting a whole trip or a single stop, which allows you to manually reorder the stops on a vehicle. Do this by clicking the blue arrow button found at the top of the Scheduled Trips sub-window. In the example below, the window is following the legacy behavior of selecting the whole trip.

You can then click the blue arrow button to select just one of the trip’s stops. This allows you to make small adjustments to the order of the vehicle’s stops and improve its schedule.
