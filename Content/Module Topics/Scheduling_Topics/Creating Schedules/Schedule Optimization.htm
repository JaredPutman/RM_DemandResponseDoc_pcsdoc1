Schedule Optimization

The Optimization feature allows you to automatically create a full schedule with RSE. This function takes into account your various trip options and tuning parameters when optimizing the schedule. Optimization can be run for a service, vehicle, multiple services, or multiple vehicles at the same time. Whenever an optimization is used to generate a schedule, the View Summary Statistics and View Unscheduled Trip Statistics options are available on the Optimize drop-down menu. You should check these after completing an optimization.

 

You can also use RSE to supplement the scheduling of trips that you are unable to schedule manually. Once the manual process is complete, you can run an optimization to fill in the spaces still available on the vehicles.

Be Aware when Optimizing

When you perform an optimization, it is important that no one in your agency makes changes to the schedule, trips, or vehicles that are being used during the optimization.
