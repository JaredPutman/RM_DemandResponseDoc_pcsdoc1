How to Post to Dispatching and Lock

You can manually post a trip/break/run/vehicle to Dispatching, which locks the item. Multiple selection of items is also acceptable for this option. Once posted, the lock icon displays and no further modifications are allowed for that trip/break/run/vehicle. Follow the procedure below:

In Unscheduled Trips, select a trip/break/run/vehicle or multiple items and then either:

  1. Click the Post to Dispatching and Lock button on the toolbar to post and lock.
OR
  1. Right-click and select Post to Dispatching and Lock from the menu as shown below.

 

This option is only available to customers who are set up for manual posting at installation. If you have automatic posting set up for your system, you will not see the icon for posting display on the Scheduling window.
