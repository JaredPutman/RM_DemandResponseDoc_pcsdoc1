Utilizing the Appointment Tab

You can also add appointment information for a new trip by clicking on the Appointment tab at the bottom of the “Add New Trip” window and completing the following steps:

  1. Select a customer to add appointment information for by performing a search. You can search for the customer’s last name, full name, social security number, phone number, or the internal identification number assigned to that customer by your agency.
  2. Enter the name of the person who arranged the trip for your customer.
  3. Enter the phone number of the trip’s drop-off location.
  4. Enter any notes about how the trip was arranged.
  5. In the Appointment Check section of the screen:
  1. Enter the name of the person who was called for the pre-appointment check.
  2. Enter the date for when this call was made.
  3. Select either Yes or No for the outcome of this call.
  4. Enter any notes for the appointment check.
  1. In the Payment Plan section of the screen:
  1. Enter the information of the payment plan the customer used for this appointment.
  2. Enter the date of the payment plan.
  1. When you are finished, click Save and Return.
