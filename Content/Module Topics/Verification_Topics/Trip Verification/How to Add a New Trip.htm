How to Add a New Trip

If you find that a trip is missing from the original schedule or manifest, you can add it from the Verification screen by completing the following steps:

  1. Click the New Trip button. The screen that appears is very similar to the creation screen found in the Trips module.

  1. Select a customer to add a trip for by performing a search. You can search for the customer’s last name, full name, social security number, phone number, or the internal identification number assigned to that customer by your agency.
  2. Enter the address of the trip’s pickup and drop-off. You can also select this information by choosing one of the customer’s favorite trips.
  3. Enter the time of the trip’s pickup and drop-off.
  4. Enter the funding source of the trip.
  5. Select the customer’s mobility type.
  6. Click Save and Continue as you go along to keep a record of your work.

The following information is optional when adding a new trip, but should be entered for accurate reporting:

  1. Enter the odometer reading of the vehicle at the start and end of the trip.
  2. In the Car Seat Count box, enter a number for any extra car seats if they were needed.
  3. Select any assistance needs that applied to the customer.
  4. Select the purpose of the trip.
  5. Select the trip’s billing category.
  6. Select the service applied to this trip.
  7. Select what type of trip this was.
  8. Select the fare type that was applied to this trip.
  9. To enter a fare adjustment, complete the following steps:
  1. In the Customer Pay Collected – Fare Type fields, enter the correct fare(s) for each passenger. These fields display the adjusted customer pay by default.
  2. Enter zero for any customer pay amounts that apply to cancelled trips and no shows.
  3. This will make the Customer Pay collected box at the top of the Verification Screen reconcile correctly with cash collected and assist with the Invoicing and Rider Under Payment Reports.
  1. Enter an amount for an additional charge if one was applied to this trip.
  2. Enter the number and mobility type for any attendants who accompanied your customer on their trip.
  3. Enter the number and mobility type for any guests who accompanied your customer on their trip.
  4. Enter Trip, Customer, and Driver comments for the trip if there are any to record.
  5. If any of the following applied to this trip, check the appropriate boxes:
  • Late? – Was the customer late?
  • Verbal Abuse – Did the customer commit verbal abuse during the trip?
  • NoShow – Did the customer fail to show up for their trip?
  • Service Refusal – Did the customer refuse the service?
  • Service Animal – Did the customer have a service animal accompany them on the trip?
  • Physical Abuse – Did the customer commit physical abuse during the trip?
  1. When you are finished, click Save and Return.
