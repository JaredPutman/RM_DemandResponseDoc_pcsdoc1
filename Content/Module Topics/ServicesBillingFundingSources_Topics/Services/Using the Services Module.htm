Using the Services Module

The Services Module window allows you to view and edit the services you have configured. You can also use the Service Abuses tab to associate configured service abuses to the services you have set up for your agency.

Example

A transit agency offers one service for riders within the city limits and one for the suburbs outside of town. Trips for the suburb service are generally longer and can be more difficult to schedule than the city service. To account for this, the agency gives the suburb service a longer max trip time and larger PU/DO windows. These settings cause the software to treat the two service’s trips differently to match their typical operations.
