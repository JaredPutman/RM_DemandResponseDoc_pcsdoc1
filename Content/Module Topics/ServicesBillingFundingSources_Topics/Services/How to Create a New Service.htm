How to Create a New Service

  1. Start by clicking the New button on the toolbar.
  2. Enter a Name for this service (Required).

Note

The Statistics section of the screen is for review after a service has been created and used. An explanation of this feature is located in this chapter (p.1).

  1. Configure the Settings based on the service’s planned operations and requirements. It is important to note that these scheduling options override any defaults configured in the Settings module. The next section fully explains each setting.

  1. Assign vehicle pools to this service by completing the following steps. This helps ensure that customers are scheduled to their correct vehicles.
  1. Select a vehicle pool from the Unassigned Pools column on the left.
  2. Click the Add button to move the selected pool into the Assigned Pools column on the right.
  3. This assigns the vehicle pool to the service.
  1. Select a National Transit Database (NTD) Category Identifier (if one applies) by clicking the corresponding checkboxes. Your choices are ADA, Sponsored, or Private Pay and you do have the option to selecting multiple NTD categories.
  2. When you have entered all the necessary information for this service, click Save on the toolbar.
