How to Create a Billing Rule

RouteMatch uses billing rules to calculate the expected customer pay and funding source cost when trips are saved and posted. The system also utilizes these rules when printing a driver manifest, when calculating customer pay on demand in the Dispatching module, and when verifying trips. In order to compute billing, you must define a rule’s cost, calculation method, and its associated trip details.

  1. On the toolbar, click the New button and choose whether this a new rule for trips or runs. This opens the Settings tab of the Billing Module.

  1. You then need to make configurations and enter relevant information in the main areas of the screen according to your agency’s policies. Each of these areas are fully in the next few sections below.
  1. General Information (p.1) – Enter details of the billing rule that your agency uses to identify and organize it.
  2. Expected Customer Pay (p.1) – Determine the amount of money that the customer is expected to pay when they take a trip with this billing rule.
  3. Compute Non-Customer Pay Cost (p.1) – Define the amount of money that a customer’s funding source pays when they take trip.
  4. Computation Applies to (p.1) – Configure criteria to specify which trips, funding sources, etc. apply to this billing rule.
  1. Once you have addressed these items, click the Save button on the toolbar.
