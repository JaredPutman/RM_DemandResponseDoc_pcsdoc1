How to View Customer Trip History

  1. In the Customers module, navigate to the customer you wish to view the trip history of.
  2. Click the Customer Trip History button located on the tool bar.
  3. The customer’s trip history displays in a new window.
  4. If you wish to view a different customer’s trip history, type their name in to the box at the top of the screen and click the drop-down button. Then select the customer you want from the names that appear.

 

You can view the Customers Trip History in the Customers, Dispatching, Trips, Scheduling, and Verification modules.
