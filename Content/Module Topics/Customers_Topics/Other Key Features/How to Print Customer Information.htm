How to Print Customer Information

  1. In the Customers module, navigate to the customer you wish to print information for.
  2. Click the Print button on the tool bar and select the "Print Customer Information" option.
  3. Select which pieces of the customer's information you would like to print out by clicking the appropriate checkboxes. Then click the Run button.

  1. A preview of the report you chose appears on the screen. You can print it out from here using the Print Report button on the toolbar.
