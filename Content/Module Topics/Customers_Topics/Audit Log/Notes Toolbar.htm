Notes Toolbar

On the Notes tool bar, you can also do the following:

  • Undo/redo changes
  • Delete the note
  • Copy the selected text to the clipboard
  • Cut the selected text to the clipboard
  • Paste text from the clipboard to the curser’s location
  • Choose the font size of the text
  • View the history of edits made to individual notes by clicking the History tab.
  • In the history tab, you can also undo changes made to notes by restoring them to previous versions.
