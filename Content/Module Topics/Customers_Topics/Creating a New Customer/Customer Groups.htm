Customer Groups

If your agency utilized RouteMatch's Group Trip functionality, you must add customers to groups in the Customers module. Once your customers are sorted into their groups, you can book a group trip in the Trips module. This effectively allows you to create a trip and apply it to multiple customers at the same time.

Refer to the Trips Module User Guide for more information on booking Group Trips.

Please keep the following items in mind when setting up the customer groups. Failure to do so may cause errors.

  • All customers in a group must have a default funding source set.
  • All customers in a group must have their home addresses geocoded.
  1. In the Customers module, click the Group Management button on the toolbar. The screen that appears contains a list of all of your agency's configured groups.

  1. Select one of your agency's groups and then click the Next button.

  1. Select the customer(s) you would like to add to the group. You can use the "control" or "shift" buttons on your keyboard to select multiple customers.

  1. Click the Add to Group button to add the selected customers to the group. Notice that the customers move from the "Available" column to the "Group" column.

You can assign the same customer to multiple groups.

  1. Click the Done button once you add all of the needed customers to the group.
