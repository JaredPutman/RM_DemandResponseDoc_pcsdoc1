Travel Restriction

An alternative function of this tab can be to keep track of the doctor information for the customer.

  1. Click the Travel Restriction tab and fill in the following fields:
  • Travel Restriction – Click this box to activate a travel restriction for the customer.
  • Restriction Comments – When the Travel Restriction box is checked, this box becomes accessible. Enter information in this field related to the customer’s travel limitations.
  • Supervisory Override Required – Select this checkbox to make it mandatory to have a supervisor override the travel restriction each time a trip is booked for the customer. This creates a pop-up that displays whenever a user creates a new trip for the customer. The popup shows the restriction that is in place in the Restriction Comments box.
  1. Click Save on the Tool Bar.
