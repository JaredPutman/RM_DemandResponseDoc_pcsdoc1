Emergency Contacts

You can enter Multiple Emergency Contacts in this sub-tab:

  1. Click the Emergency Contacts tab and fill in the following fields:
  • Name – The first name, middle initial, and last name of the customer’s emergency contact.
  • Relationship – Select the relationship of the contact to the customer. These are set in the Settings under Customer Options>Relationships.
  • Telephone – Telephone number of the contact.
  • Extension – Telephone extension of the contact.
  • Comments – Enter any extra information for the contact not covered in the other fields.
  1. Click Save on the Tool Bar.
