Medical / Disability

  1. Click the Medical/Disability tab and fill in the following fields:
  • Disability Type (Required) – Select an option that describes the customer’s disability.
  • Doctor First/Last Name – Enter the first and last name of the customer’s doctor.
  • Doctor Phone/Extension – Phone number and extension of the customer’s doctor.
  • Comments – Enter any extra data not covered by the other fields.
  1. Click Save on the toolbar.
