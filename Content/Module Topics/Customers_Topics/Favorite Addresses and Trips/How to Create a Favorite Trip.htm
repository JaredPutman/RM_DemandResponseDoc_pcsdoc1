How to Create a Favorite Trip

  1. Choose a customer and navigate to the Fav Trips tab.
  2. Click one of the black down-facing arrow and choose a category for the trip (Medical, School, Shopping, etc.). These are established in the Settings under Customer Options > Favorite Trips Categories.
  3. Click Select to bring up the Previous Trips screen.
  4. Click the appropriate trip to highlight it.
  5. Click Select Trip, and the information populates onto the Favorite Trips screen.
  6. Repeat this process for each of the customer’s favorite trips.
  7. Click Save on the Tool Bar.
