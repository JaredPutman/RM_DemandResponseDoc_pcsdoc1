How to Assign an Abuse to a Suspension

To assign a service abuse to a customer’s suspension, complete the following steps:

  1. Double-click a customer’s suspension. This displays the Suspension Line Items screen.

  1. Select an abuse currently assigned to the customer from the drop-down menu in the first field.
  2. Verify that the information that displays in correct.
  3. Click Save and Return on the tool bar.  This assigns the abuse to the selected customer suspension.
