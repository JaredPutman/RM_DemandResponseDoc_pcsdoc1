How to Add a Customer Suspension

  1. Click the customer you wish to add a suspension to and navigate to the Suspension tab.

  1. Click the New Item button at the top half of the screen.
  2. Enter the Start Date of the suspension period.
  3. Enter a Grace Period. This is the number of days after the start date that the suspension takes effect.
  4. Enter an End Date for when the suspension stops.
  5. Select a Reason for the suspension from the drop-down list.
  6. Enter any additional information for this reason.
  7. Choose the Services to suspend a customer from. Make this choice from a multi-select dropdown box listing all the services from your agency. Use the All/No Services option to choose all of your services, or to clear the list and choose none of them.
  8. The Assigned By field automatically populates with the user currently logged in to RouteMatch.
  9. The Assigned by DateTime field automatically populates with the current date and time.
  10. Select a Level for the suspension.  At each level, the customer is suspended for a longer period of time.
  11. Click Save and Return on the Tool Bar.

Edit a suspension by selecting the field you wish to edit and make your changes. Be sure to Save your work. Delete a suspension by selecting the appropriate suspension and click the Delete button.
