How to Change the Stop Date or Time of a Trip

  1. Click in the Stop Time field you want to change. Notice that the field is highlighted and buttons display to the right of the field as shown below. Click the field you want to change.

 

For agencies that operate a 24/7 schedule, stop times after midnight will be listed in addition to the stops for the selected dates.

  1. Adjust the date or time as necessary.
  2. Click Save to update the trip record.
