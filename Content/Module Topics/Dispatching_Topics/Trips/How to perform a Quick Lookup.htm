How to perform a Quick Lookup

  1. On the Quick Lookup screen, enter your search criteria in the Reservation/Customer box.
  2. Select the type of search you want to make.
  3. Click Lookup. If you are searching on the Last Name and multiple customers match that name or partial name, the Customer Lookup dialog appears, allowing you to select the customer.

  1. Review the returned trips in your Trip Records list. Select the trip you want to view or edit.

  2. Once selected, the Real-Time Trip Statistics for that trip appear below the list. Notice in the graphic below, the information in this section is read-only and cannot be edited. The actual pick-up and drop-off times are displayed along with the scheduled pick-up and drop-off time.

  1. The system also loads any customer service comments and the trip’s details at the bottom of the screen. You can edit these items by following the steps in the next section.
  2. To view the trip in a Dispatch Data View, click Go to or use the keyboard shortcut Alt+G (if this has been configured in the Settings module.) See the section Configuring Quick Lookup for information on how to configure this.
