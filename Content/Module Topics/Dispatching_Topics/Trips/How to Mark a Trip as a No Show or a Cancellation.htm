How to Mark a Trip as a No Show or a Cancellation

  1. Open the desired data view, enter a Customer Name in the search field, and click the Search button or expand the grouping in the data view to display the customer trips.
  2. Locate the trip in the Dispatching grid and check the checkbox in either the No Show or Cancellation column for that trip. Once you click outside of the No Show/Cancellation field, the system will check for trips that are chained to the trip you just marked.

 

Trips chained to the marked trip will display in a pop-up, as shown below. Trips completed or that have an MDC status later than "Trip Scheduled" will not be considered.

  1. Select trip(s) from the pop-up that you want to be deleted along with the original trip by checking the checkbox in the Select column next to those trips.
  2. Click Save and Return to complete the action.
  3. Select Save in the Dispatching toolbar to mark the original trip as No Show/Cancellation.
