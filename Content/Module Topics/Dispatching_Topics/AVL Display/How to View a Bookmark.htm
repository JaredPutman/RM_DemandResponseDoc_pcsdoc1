How to View a Bookmark

Navigate to one of your bookmarks by completing the following steps.  You can jump to your default bookmark by clicking the Bookmark icon. This default is configured in the Organize Bookmarks window, which is explained in the next section.

  1. Click the arrow next to the bookmark icon to display a list of your bookmarks in a drop-down menu.
  2. Select the bookmark you wish to view.
  3. The map then loads the bookmarked location.
