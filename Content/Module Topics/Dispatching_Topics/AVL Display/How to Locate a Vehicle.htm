How to Locate a Vehicle

After you have loaded the necessary vehicles on the AVL map, you can navigate to a specific vehicle by completing the following steps:

  1. On the Dispatching window, click the AVL Display tab.
  2. In the vehicle/filter pane, select the Vehicle from the list.
  3. Click Find on the Vehicle List toolbar. The located vehicle displays with a yellow circle around it on the map. In the Vehicle List, the vehicle displays with a star beside it if the vehicle was successfully found.
