AVL Symbols Tab

This tab controls the AVL symbols that appear on your map when viewing ALV data. The sub-tabs at the bottom of the screen are used to specify how you want to view scheduled and active data and the order of preference for drawing routes on the map.

The Scheduled Routes sub-tab allows you to display various parts of the paratransit and fixed routes you are viewing in the AVL.

The Drawing Order Preference sub-tab allows you to display the vehicle icons on top or the route labels on top.
