Duplicate Customer Check

RouteMatch is able to alert a user when they create a duplicate customer record in the Customers module. It does this by checking the first name, last name, and date of birth for all new customer records. If all three of those match another record, then an alert displays to inform you that a customer already exists who has that information.

You then need to decide whether or not to save the duplicate. Click Yes to save, or No to cancel.

If your agency does not record customers' date of birth, the system only checks their first name and last name.
