MTData Integration

RouteMatch now integrates with the MTData fleet management solution and allows data to pass between both systems. Agencies that offer a taxi service and use MTData can take advantage of this integration to do the following:

  • Track taxi locations
  • Receive trip updates from drivers and display them in Dispatching
  • Create day-of and future trips and send them through the MTData API

If your agency would like to take advantage of this integration, please contact RouteMatch staff.
