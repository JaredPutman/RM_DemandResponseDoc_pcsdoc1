Service Exceptions

You can now restrict trip bookings for specific dates in order to adjust for holidays or other service changes. For example, if your agency is closed on Christmas, you can use this feature to prevent users from booking trips on December 25th.

How to Create a Service Exception

  1. In the Scheduling module, click the Views button on the toolbar and select the Service Exception Management option from the drop-down menu.

  1. Select the date of the service exception on the calendar screen that appears. Notice that you can click the Current Week button at the top of the screen to automatically move to the present week.

  1. Then, click the Create button on the toolbar, or right-click on the date and select the Create New Exception option. The Trip Exception Management screen then displays.

  1. Begin configuring the service exception by entering a Name and Description.

  1. Decide what to do with the trips that currently exist on the selected date. You have the following options:
  • No Actions - Make no changes to the trips.
  • Cancel All Trips - Cancel all of the existing trips for the selected date.
  • Select Trips To Cancel - Select specific trips that you would like to cancel. If you select this option: click the ellipsis, select the trips to cancel in the screen that appears, and then click the Cancel Selected button. You then need to confirm the cancellation.

If you choose to cancel any trips, please keep in mind that this operation cannot be reversed.

  1. Select a trip booking option to determine any limitations for creating trips during the service exception. You have the following options:
  • No Restrictions - Users can still book trips during the service exceptions as normal.
  • No New Booking - Users are blocked from booking new trips during the service exception.
  • Require Supervisor Override - Users can book trips during the service exception, but they require a supervisor override.
  1. To configure the service exception's pop-up message , click the checkbox and then enter the message's text. This pop-up displays when a user attempts to book a trip on the selected date.
  2. Once the exception is configured correctly, click the Save button at the bottom of the screen. Notice that it now appears on the calendar shaded in one of the following colors to indicate the trip booking limitation.

  • Green - No Restrictions
  • Red - No New Booking
  • Yellow - Require Supervisor Override

How to Edit or Delete a Service Exception

If you need to edit or delete a service exception (if it was made in error perhaps) you can do so by selecting it on the calendar. Then, click the Edit or Delete button on the toolbar, or right-click on the date and select Edit or Delete from there. At this point, you either edit the exception and save it, or confirm the deletion and remove it from the system.
