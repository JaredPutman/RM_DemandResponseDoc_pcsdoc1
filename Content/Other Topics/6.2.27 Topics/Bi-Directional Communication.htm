Bi-Directional Communication

This version of RouteMatch contains the initial release of the Bi-Directional Communication (BDC) system. Please contact your account manager if you are interested in BDC.

As a demand response dispatcher, BDC allows you to verbally communicate with your agency's drivers to help resolve any issues that occur during service. For example, if a driver reports a no-show, you may want to call them to confirm they are at the proper pick-up location. This solution contains the following features:

  • BDC Calls - Calls between a dispatcher and a driver. Both parties must press a button in order to speak and then release it to hear the other person.
  • Broadcast Calls - Announcements from the dispatcher that broadcast over the external speaker found on a vehicle. These calls can be made to one driver, or several at once.
  • Covert Alarms - Alerts triggered by the driver to inform you of a emergency situations on their vehicle.
  • Event Records - Reports to note any necessary details of a BDC call that members of your agency may need to refer to in the future.
  • Voice Recording - The ability to record the calls between dispatchers and the drivers for future reference.
