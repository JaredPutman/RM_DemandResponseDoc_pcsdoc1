Excessive Dwell Time Alert

There is a new type of alert that informs users of any vehicles that dwell too long at a stop without departing. This can inform your staff of potential issues happening out in the field, so you can correct them in a timely matter. When configuring this alert you need to define the following thresholds:

  • Excessive Demand Dwell Time
  • Excessive Dwell Factor
  • Load Time
  • No Show Timer
